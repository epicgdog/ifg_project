# ForgeReach V1 — Sample Outreach

This document shows ten representative 3-step sequences the pipeline produces for the bundled `data/sample_contacts.csv` roster: five blue-collar owners and five referral advocates (fractional CFOs, wealth advisors, insurance brokers, EOS implementers, CEPA/exit advisors, bankers).

The examples below were produced in `--dry-run` mode, which bypasses the LLM and returns deterministic placeholder copy seeded from Kory Mitchell's voice profile. In a live run, the same contact context is fed to OpenRouter along with the MASTER persona few-shot examples to generate bespoke copy.

---

## Jake Morrison — Owner at Morrison Roofing

**Audience:** owner &nbsp;&nbsp; **Qualification tier:** high

<details>
<summary><b>Step 1 — Founder-to-founder intro on your market</b></summary>

> Jake, I am Kory Mitchell. I built my career in blue-collar businesses and now advise founders through IFG on growth and eventual exits. Your company stood out to me because operator-led businesses in your space are creating real value right now. Open to a short founder-to-founder call to compare what is working in your market?
>
> - Kory Mitchell

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Step 2 — Two practical moves owners are using</b></summary>

> Jake, one thing I hear from owners repeatedly is they are strong operationally but have not translated that into a clear exit-ready story. We help tighten that gap while the business keeps growing. If helpful, I can send two practical moves owners are using this quarter. Want me to send them?
>
> - Kory Mitchell

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Step 3 — Twenty minutes on 12-24 month leverage</b></summary>

> Jake, I know timing has to be right for this kind of conversation. If now is not ideal, no issue. If it is, we can keep it simple and spend 20 minutes on where your business sits today and what could improve leverage over the next 12-24 months. Worth putting on the calendar?
>
> - Kory Mitchell

</details>

## Angela Price — Fractional CFO at Price Advisory Group

**Audience:** referral_advocate &nbsp;&nbsp; **Qualification tier:** high

<details>
<summary><b>Step 1 — Comparing notes on owner profiles</b></summary>

> Angela, I run IFG and spend most of my time with blue-collar founders thinking about growth and exit timing. Your work puts you close to owners making big decisions, so I wanted to introduce myself directly. Would you be open to a short call to compare notes on what these owners are asking for most right now?
>
> - Kory Mitchell

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Step 2 — A pattern advisors pass to clients</b></summary>

> Angela, one thing we see often is owners waiting too long to prepare for transition, then losing leverage. We help them tighten operations and narrative before they ever go to market. If useful, I can share a simple pattern we use that advisors pass to clients early. Worth sending over?
>
> - Kory Mitchell

</details>

<summary><b>Step 3 — One-page overview for your team</b></summary>

>
> - Kory Mitchell

</details>

## How these were generated

Examples above use the deterministic `--dry-run` templates in `src/messaging.py:_generate_dry_run_sequence()` — safe for local development, CI, and sample previews without burning tokens. The full pipeline in live mode composes the `MASTER.md` persona (now including Section 6 few-shot examples) with the `data/voice_profile.json` tone constraints and the `src/messaging.py:build_sequence_prompt()` scaffolding, and calls OpenRouter for every contact. Subjects and bodies are validated by `src/validators.py` (word count, taboo phrases, signature presence, subject-line format) before the row is written to `out/campaign_ready.csv` and the trimmed `out/instantly_campaign.csv` export.
